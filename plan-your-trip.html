Hamad International Airport (DOH)

Qatar's modern gateway — facilities, duty free, lounges and transport links

About Hamad International Airport

Hamad International Airport is a major regional hub with passenger lounges, excellent retail and dining options, family facilities, and efficient transfer connections. It is located roughly 15–20 minutes from central Doha (depending on traffic).

Duty Free & Shopping

The airport's duty-free offers a wide selection of perfumes, cosmetics, luxury goods, electronics, and local souvenirs. Opening hours generally correspond with flight schedules.

Getting To & From the Airport

Options include Doha Metro (Red Line), taxis (Karwa / ride-hailing), hotel transfers and car rental pick-up points at the terminal.

Visa & Entry

Quick guide to visitor entry requirements

Do I need a visa?

Visa rules depend on nationality. Many countries are eligible for visa on arrival or an electronic travel authorization (ETA). Check official government sources for exact requirements before travel.

Common visa types

  • Visa on Arrival — available to certain nationalities at the airport.
  • Pre-approved eVisa / ETA — apply online before travel if required.
  • Transit Visa — for short layovers, depending on eligibility.

Documents you may need

Valid passport (6+ months), return/onward ticket, proof of accommodation, and enough funds for your stay. Always verify conditions with your embassy or airline.
