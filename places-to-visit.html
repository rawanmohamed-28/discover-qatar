Places to Visit in Qatar

From traditional souqs to world-class malls and fine dining, Qatar offers endless experiences for every traveler

Start Exploring

Discover Top Attractions

Select a category to explore Qatar's best destinations

IDAM by Alain Ducasse

Located in the Museum of Islamic Art, this fine dining restaurant offers modern Mediterranean cuisine with stunning views of Doha's skyline. Type: Luxury. Highlights: Michelin-starred chef, romantic ambiance, art museum location.

Tea Time

Doha-born restaurant chain has been serving tasty, on-the-go bites and drinks, all made from the freshest, top quality ingredients.Type: Budget-Friendly. Highlights: Traditional karak.

SMAT Qatar

Contemporary Qatari cuisine with a modern twist, offering a taste of local hospitality and culture. Type: Luxury. Highlights: Traditional dishes reimagined, cultural experience, elegant setting.

Damasca One Restaurant

A popular spot in Souq Waqif serving delicious Syrian cuisine at affordable prices. Type: Budget-friendly. Highlights: Shawarma, grilled meats, authentic atmosphere, family-friendly.

Souq Waqif

One of Doha's most famous markets, filled with spices, textiles, souvenirs, and traditional Qatari handicrafts. Highlights: Traditional restaurants, shisha cafes, and the Falcon Souq.

Gold Souq

Located near Souq Waqif, it showcases intricate gold jewelry and precious gems — a true treasure trove. Highlights: Custom jewelry design, traditional Arabic gold pieces, and competitive pricing.

Falcon Souq

A unique market where visitors can see traditional falconry gear and even live falcons — part of Qatar's heritage. Highlights: Falcon hospital visits, traditional hunting equipment, and cultural insights.

Souq Al Wakrah

A beautifully restored traditional market in the coastal town of Al Wakrah with authentic architecture. Highlights: Waterfront views, art galleries, traditional crafts, and peaceful atmosphere away from the city.

Mall of Qatar

Qatar's largest mall with over 500 stores including luxury brands, dining options, and entertainment. Highlights: IMAX cinema, family entertainment center, and the Galeries Lafayette.

Villaggio Mall

A Venice-themed shopping destination with luxury brands and charming architecture. Highlights: Indoor canal with gondola rides, Olympic-size ice rink, and Italian-inspired ambiance.

Doha Festival City

A massive entertainment and shopping complex offering family fun and retail therapy. Highlights: IKEA, VOX Cinemas, Angry Birds World theme park, and SnowDunes indoor snow park.

City Center Mall

A popular shopping hub in West Bay with international brands and dining options. Highlights: Carrefour hypermarket, family entertainment, and convenient parking.

Al Hazm Mall

An ultra-luxury shopping destination inspired by Ottoman and Andalusian architecture. Highlights: High-end fashion brands like Gucci and Louis Vuitton, fine dining, and stunning marble interiors.

Vendôme Mall

A luxury shopping mall in Lusail with a French-inspired elegant design. Highlights: Designer boutiques, gourmet restaurants, and a sophisticated atmosphere.

Tawar Mall

A convenient neighborhood mall offering everyday shopping and services. Highlights: Lulu Hypermarket, affordable fashion stores, and family-friendly dining.

Ezdan Mall

A popular community mall known for its variety of shops and entertainment. Highlights: Al Meera supermarket, fashion outlets, children's play areas, and food court.

Mina District (Old Doha Port)

A historic waterfront area showcasing Doha's maritime heritage with traditional dhows and cultural landmarks. Highlights: Traditional wooden dhows, fish market, waterfront promenade, authentic local atmosphere, heritage architecture.

Museum of Islamic Art (MIA)

An architectural masterpiece designed by I.M. Pei, showcasing 1,400 years of Islamic art and artifacts from three continents. Highlights: Iconic building design, rare manuscripts, ceramics and textiles, waterfront park, MIA Park, IDAM restaurant.

National Museum of Qatar

A rose-shaped architectural wonder designed by Jean Nouvel, telling the story of Qatar from ancient times to the present day. Highlights: Desert rose-inspired design, immersive exhibits, heritage galleries, lagoon views, Jiwan restaurant by Alain Ducasse.

Katara Cultural Village

Qatar's premier cultural destination featuring art galleries, theaters, restaurants, and a beautiful beach along the Corniche. Highlights: Amphitheater, mosques, art exhibitions, multicultural restaurants, beach access, cultural festivals and events.

Qatar National Library

A stunning modern library designed by OMA, offering vast collections, study spaces, and cultural programs in Education City. Highlights: Futuristic architecture, over 1 million books, Heritage Library collection, free membership, quiet study areas, digital resources.

3-2-1 Qatar Olympic and Sports Museum

An interactive sports museum celebrating Olympic heritage and Qatar's sporting achievements with engaging exhibits. Highlights: Olympic torch collection, interactive displays, World Cup 2022 exhibits, sports memorabilia, Hall of Athletes, hands-on activities.
